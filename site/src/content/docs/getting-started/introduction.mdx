Arcagrad is a self-hosted server and reader for **comics, manga, and books**. Point it
at a folder, and it becomes a private library you (and anyone you invite) can read
from anywhere — with covers, tags, search, and reading progress that syncs across
your devices.

It's built in Rust and ships as a single Docker image, and it's designed to stay fast
even as your collection grows to more than **100,000 items**.

## Core ideas

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="One library for everything">
    Keep comics, manga, standalone works, series, and EPUB books together. Arcagrad
    automatically chooses the appropriate reading experience for each item.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Keeps your original files intact">
    Arcagrad never rewrites the contents of an existing book or archive. Library
    information is stored separately and can be rebuilt by scanning your collection
    again.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-user from day one">
    Accounts gate access, and reading progress, favorites, ratings, and
    recommendations are all per user.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Batteries included">
    Optional plugins can add metadata, browse external catalogs, and track releases.
    Search, recommendations, and multi-user reading are built into the server.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
